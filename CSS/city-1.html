Hyderabad








Hyderabad is the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Telangana and the de jure capital of Andhra Pradesh.
With an average altitude of 542 metres (1,778 ft), much of Hyderabad is situated on hilly terrain around artificial lakes, including the
Hussain Sagar lake, predating the city's founding, in the north of the city centre.Hyderabad is
the fourth-most populous city in India.It the sixth-most populous metropolitan area in India.
